# Support & SLAs

_Category: Support · Last updated: 2026-06-18_

## Support channels

Support is available by email on Pro, with priority support on Business and a dedicated CSM on Enterprise. The Free plan uses community support.

## Response-time SLAs

Business priority support targets a first response within 4 business hours. Enterprise targets within 1 hour and includes a 99.9% uptime SLA. Free and Pro do not carry a specific response-time SLA.

## Escalation & tickets

Issues that cannot be resolved immediately are escalated with a ticket in the format NIM- followed by five digits. Account security, suspected fraud, data loss, and legal or privacy requests are escalated as priority to the appropriate team.

## Service status

Service status and incident history are published at status.nimbushq.example.
