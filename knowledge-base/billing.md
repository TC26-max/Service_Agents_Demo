# Billing & Refunds

_Category: Billing · Last updated: 2026-05-20_

## Refund policy

Paid plans (Pro and Business) are refundable within 14 days of a charge. Refunds requested after 14 days are not covered by the standard policy and are reviewed case by case by the billing team.

## Downgrades & credit

When you downgrade an annual plan, the unused balance is applied as prorated account credit toward future invoices.

## Invoices & receipts

Invoices and receipts are available under Settings, Billing, Invoices. Workspace owners and billing admins can download a PDF invoice for any past charge.

## Free trial

New teams can start a 14-day free trial of the Business plan. No credit card is required to begin the trial.

## Payment methods

Nimbus accepts major credit cards. Payment changes are made by the workspace owner under Settings, Billing. Support agents never collect or process card details.
