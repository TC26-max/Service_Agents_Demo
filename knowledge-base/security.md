# Security & Compliance

_Category: Security · Last updated: 2026-06-10_

## Certifications

Nimbus is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. A current SOC 2 report is available to customers under NDA on request.

## Encryption

All customer data is encrypted at rest with AES-256 and in transit with TLS 1.2 or higher.

## HIPAA

HIPAA support, including a signed BAA, is available as an Enterprise add-on. It is not available on the Free, Pro, or Business plans.

## Data residency

Data is hosted in the United States by default. EU data residency is available on Enterprise.

## Access & audit

Access to production systems is restricted, logged, and reviewed. Audit logs of workspace activity are available on Enterprise.
