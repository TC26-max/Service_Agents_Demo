# Accounts, SSO & Admin

_Category: Accounts · Last updated: 2026-05-30_

## Login & password reset

If you cannot sign in, use the self-service password reset at nimbushq.example/reset. A reset link is emailed to the address on file.

## Single sign-on

On Business, SSO with Google or Microsoft is configured by a workspace admin under Settings, Security. On Enterprise, SAML SSO and SCIM provisioning are available.

## Roles & permissions

Members can be assigned roles: owner, admin, member, or guest. Only owners and admins can change billing, manage members, or change workspace settings.

## Ownership transfer

Transferring workspace ownership is a sensitive change. For security it is completed by the support team after verifying the current owner out-of-band; it is not performed in chat.

## Managing seats

Admins can add or remove seats under Settings, Members. Adding seats may change the amount of the next invoice.
