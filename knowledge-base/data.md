# Data Export & Portability

_Category: Product · Last updated: 2026-05-05_

## Exporting data

All paid plans can export data to CSV or JSON from any project or list. A full account export across all workspaces is available on Business and Enterprise.

## What is included

Exports include tasks, comments, custom fields, and attachment metadata. Large exports are emailed as a downloadable archive when ready.

## Data deletion requests

To request deletion of your data under GDPR or similar laws, contact support; these requests are handled by the privacy team and are not processed in chat.
