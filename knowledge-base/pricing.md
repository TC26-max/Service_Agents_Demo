# Pricing & Plans

_Category: Billing · Last updated: 2026-06-01_

## Free plan

The Free plan is $0 and supports up to 5 users, 3 active projects, basic tasks and lists, and 100 MB of storage. It includes community support. Teams can upgrade to Pro at any time.

## Pro plan

Pro is $12 per user/month billed annually, or $14 month-to-month. It includes unlimited projects, dashboards, timeline and Gantt views, 25 GB storage per user, the core integrations, 50 automation runs per month, and email support.

## Business plan

Business is $22 per user/month billed annually, or $26 month-to-month. It includes everything in Pro plus unlimited automations, workload management, custom fields, guest access, SSO with Google or Microsoft, admin controls, 100 GB storage per user, and priority support.

## Enterprise plan

Enterprise uses custom pricing. It adds SAML SSO with SCIM provisioning, audit logs, a 99.9% uptime SLA, a dedicated customer success manager, onboarding and training, and optional SOC 2, GDPR, and HIPAA add-ons. Contact sales for a quote.

## Billing cycle & discounts

Annual billing is discounted versus monthly, roughly two months free. Published discounts are limited to the annual discount and a 30% discount for verified nonprofits and educational institutions. No other discounts are offered.
